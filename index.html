About & how to play

What is 3D Pinball Space Cadet?

3D Pinball for Windows – Space Cadet is the iconic pinball game bundled with Windows back in the 95/XP era. You control two flippers to keep the ball in play, complete space-cadet missions and rack up the highest score you can. This version runs entirely in your browser — no download or install required.

Controls

  • Space — hold and release to pull the plunger and launch the ball.
  • Z — left flipper · / or . — right flipper.
  • X — nudge the table (careful — too much triggers TILT).
  • Click the game screen once to enable sound & music.
  • Finish a game and your score is submitted to the leaderboard automatically (sign-in required).
